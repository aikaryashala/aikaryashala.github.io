Vision

"The purpose of AI-Karyashala is to nurture youth to be joyful, inspired, focused and competent. We want our students to blossom into strong engineers who build our nation Bharat."

With the advent of Gen-AI, the way software is built has completely changed. But some things still remain timeless — strong fundamentals, problem-solving ability, fluid expression, algorithmic thinking, deep understanding of systems and networks, and debugging with clarity.

At AI-Karyashala, we nurture students to become AI-Native Engineers who can build for the modern AI era while staying deeply grounded in core engineering principles, curiosity and lifelong learning. ( జీవితాంత + విద్యా + అర్థీ )

We currently run the AI-Native Job Ready Program, designed to prepare students for the modern AI-driven software industry.

In our first batch, 20 out of 26 students got placed.
